# Context: Weekly Changelog Generation

**Goal:** Generate a review-ready weekly changelog draft for `changelog-agent`.
**Scope:** Summarize commits from the past 7 days on `main` branch. Group by category and format for `CHANGELOG.md` or `releases/` draft.
**Assumptions:** Commits since 7 days ago.
**Definition of Done:**
- Changes are collected and categorized.
- `CHANGELOG.md` (if exists) is updated or a release draft is created.
- Review ready format achieved.
